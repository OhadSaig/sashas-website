Our Story

Two concepts,
one neighbourhood

Sasha's began as a simple idea: bring the warmth of Italian craft and the ritual of specialty coffee to the Ballsbridge community. Tucked along Shelbourne Road, we opened our doors with a wood-fired oven, a commitment to honest ingredients, and a love for our neighbours.

Today, Sasha's operates as two connected spaces — the Pizzeria, serving Neapolitan-style pizza and pasta for lunch and dinner, and the Coffee Bar (our "Therapy Window"), your essential morning stop from 7:30am.

🔥
Wood-Fired Craft Every pizza baked at 485°C in our traditional wood-fired oven — perfection in under 90 seconds
☕
The Therapy Window Our specialty coffee bar — open from 7:30am on weekdays, serving your daily ritual
🇮🇪
Irish Quality 100% Irish beef, locally sourced where possible — we care about where our food comes from

The Oven

485°C of Neapolitan
tradition

Our wood-fired oven runs at 485°C, producing a perfectly charred, leopard-spotted cornicione in under 90 seconds. Every pizza is hand-stretched, simply topped with DOP-certified San Marzano tomatoes, and finished with care passed down through generations of Italian tradition.

What We Stand For
