The Craft

485°C. Under
90 seconds. Perfect.

Our wood-fired oven runs at 485°C — the temperature Neapolitan tradition demands. The result is a perfectly charred, leopard-spotted cornicione with a soft, airy centre. Every pizza is hand-stretched, simply topped, and finished with care passed down through generations of Italian craft.
